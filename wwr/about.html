History

The founders of this company fell in love with the sport of White Water Rafting 15 years ago. They both worked during their summers in college as Guides through their local college wwr program. Once they graduated they knew they wanted to turn their passion into a business.

They started small, but were able to grow this company into the wonderful addition to outdoors activities offered in this area by finding amazing equiptment, people, and of course, rapids! they are always striving to improve and find new, fun and safe courses to float.
